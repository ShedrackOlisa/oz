Gameplay Overview

Lumines PSP presents players with a grid-based playing field where luminous squares fall from the top. These squares are composed of two colored blocks, and players must rotate and position them to create 2x2 quads of the same color. When a line sweep passes over a quad, it clears from the board, scoring points and creating space for more squares.

The game's challenge lies in managing the falling squares to create large quads efficiently, as the playing field continuously shifts and the music tempo affects the pace of gameplay. As players progress, the game introduces new block types, backgrounds, and music tracks, providing a dynamic and engaging experience.
